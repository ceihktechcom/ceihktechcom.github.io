Massaging

To massage, turn the product on, adjust the speed, and apply the massage head to the body.

Turning on and off

  1. To turn on, hold down the power button for 3 seconds.
  2. The motor starts.
  3. (If the battery indicator is solid RED): battery is depleted, charge it.
  4. To turn off, hold down the power button for 3 seconds.

Changing the speed

Massaging

Preconditions:

  1. Make sure you have selected a massage head suitable for the body part you want to massage. If you feel unsure, consult a professional.
  1. Gently apply the massage head to your body. CAUTION! Risk of injury! Do not apply to sensitive areas or areas with little muscle tissue. If you feel unwell, stop use immediately.
